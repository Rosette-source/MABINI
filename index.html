ABOUT US

MISSION

Our mission is to foster a safe, healthy, and prosperous community by providing essential services, promoting sustainable development, and encouraging active participation among all residents.

VISION

We envision Barangay Mabini as a model of community development, where every resident enjoys a high quality of life, and where our rich heritage and natural beauty are preserved for future generations.



History

Barangay Mabini, located in Gamu, Isabela, has a rich historical legacy that reflects the enduring resilience and growth of its community.

Established in the early 1900s and named after the esteemed Philippine hero Apolinario Mabini, the barangay initially thrived as an agricultural settlement.

Early residents relied heavily on farming and fishing, making use of the fertile lands and abundant resources available in the area.

Throughout the mid-20th century, Barangay Mabini underwent significant transformations. Improvements in infrastructure, such as roads and bridges, enhanced connectivity with surrounding regions, facilitating trade and commerce. The establishment of essential services like schools and healthcare facilities marked a period of substantial development, contributing to the overall well-being and progress of the barangay.

In recent decades, Barangay Mabini has continued to evolve, balancing modernization with the preservation of its cultural heritage. The growth of local businesses, community organizations, and public amenities reflects the barangay's commitment to progress.
